What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help improve your experience and allow us to analyze site usage. This policy outlines what cookies collect, how we use them, and how you can control them.

How We Use Cookies

  • • Improve website performance and load times
  • • Remember preferences and session activity
  • • Analyze traffic and interaction trends
  • • Enable security features and prevent fraud

Disabling cookies may affect your experience. We recommend keeping them enabled unless necessary.

Types of Cookies We Use

  1. • Essential Cookies: For basic functionality like navigation and form submissions.
  2. • Analytics Cookies: Track user behavior for site optimization (e.g., Google Analytics).
  3. • Functionality Cookies: Save preferences such as language or region.
  4. • Survey/Feedback Cookies: Support temporary storage for survey progress and visibility.
  5. • Third-Party Cookies: Set by services like YouTube, LinkedIn, or Google Ads on embedded content.

Disabling Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Refer to the instructions for your browser:

  • • Google Chrome
  • • Mozilla Firefox
  • • Microsoft Edge

More Information

We hope this policy clarifies our use of cookies. If you're unsure whether cookies are required for your use, it's safest to leave them enabled.
